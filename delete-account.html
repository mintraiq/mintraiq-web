Account & Data Deletion for MintrAIQ

Delete in the app

Open MintrAIQ → Go to Settings → Tap ‘Delete Account’.

Request without the app

If you no longer have the app installed, enter your registered email address below (or email support@mintraiq.com with the subject ‘Account Deletion Request’).

What gets deleted

Upon processing your request, MintrAIQ will permanently purge your user profile, stored receipt images, parsed transaction history, and subscription links from our database within 30 days.
